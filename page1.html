St. John Brigade

My first CCA in secondary school, St. John Brigade

I didn't expect myself to love this CCA as much as i do because I was in a performing arts CCA in primary school. But, I somehow ended up falling in love with this CCA and my squadmates are my best friends!

In St. John Brigade, we do learn first aid, do hours of foot drills to practise discipline, and my favourite part, PT!

Overall, I've enjoyed my time in St. John Brigade so far, and i look forward to making more memories!
